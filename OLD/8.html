Boil Water Advisory for Athens water customers in Melrose Square area (10/30/17)

Posted on Oct 30th, 2017 in News


Athens water customers in the Melrose Square area including Old Athens Road, Eads Mill Road, and Simmons Circle: There has been a major water line break on Route 20 near Melrose Square. Crews are working to repair this break. Customers in this area may be experiencing low or no water pressure. In addition, out of an abundance of caution until the water is retested, a Boil Water Advisory is in effect for customers in this area. (This does not affect in town water customers.)
