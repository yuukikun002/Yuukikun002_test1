Municipal Sales and Service Tax

Posted on Dec 16th, 2017 in News


Notice is hereby given that the Town of Athens, a municipal corporation, will hold a hearing before the final vote on a proposed ordinance, the principal object of which is the raising of revenue for the municipality. The title of such ordinance is “Municipal Sales and Service Tax.” The final vote on adoption of said proposed ordinance shall be held in the Council Chambers of the Town of Athens, Town Hall, 2020 South State Street, Athens, West Virginia on December 27, 2017 at Six o’clock (6:00) p.m. Interested parties may appear and be heard at such time with respect to the passage of the proposed ordinance. Copies of the proposed ordinance are available at the Office of the Clerk, Town Hall, in Athens.

Timothy Pike, Mayor
